Theos Seminary

Learning Platform: Head of Product Design

In 2020 I had the privilege to work with one of my favorite Theologians new online Seminary program.
‍
Nathan and Gabriel Finnochio are amazing people building a innovative platform for those wanting to earn a certificate in Theology Biblical Studies.

In January They reached out to me to come on board as their Head of Product Design on a contract basis and come up with the flow, experience, and Visual appearance for their new platform.

Team:

Myself as well as Akash Pathak worked to make their platform become a reality.

UX Research

We didn't have much guidance other than they wanted the platform to be interactive and have a Harry Potter / Lord of the Rings appeal.


Myself as well as Akash worked together to look at what other learning platforms were doing and how they decided to layout their content. We knew that the company wanted a Harry Potter feel to their platform, so we looked at some images from the movies. What we discovered was they used tones of browns, warm greys, and yellows.


We also tried a bunch of fonts types until we found one that went well with the overall look and feel of the platform and goal the company was trying to reach.
