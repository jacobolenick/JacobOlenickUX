Virtual Reality Conference

Corporate Design : Website : 3D Modeling

OneDay_VR is a Virtual Reality Conference in South Germany.  It is about new immersive technology (like Virtual Reality as well as Augmented and Mixed Reality), their challenges, approaches and everything you need to know about it. It is organized by students of Visual Communication Design and Computer Science. You can not only experience reports and discussions, about how other realities change our everyday lives, but also immerse yourself into vivid story worlds and new experiences, that students built themselves.

I was involved in organizing the very first one of this meanwhile biannual event. I designed the CD, set up a website and made an image movie. Furthermore, I was invited to present my VR project on stage and at the exhibition.
