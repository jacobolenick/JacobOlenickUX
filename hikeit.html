Hikeit

Co Hiking App: Concept

Imagine an app that's for hiking yet socializing. You can send friend requests to people only if they hiked the same trail as you on the same day. A new way to meet people and create long lasting relationships for many hikes to come.

Brand Page

My role on this product was to design the brand page. This was essentially the profile for either the company or individual selling the product or service.

UX Research

I tried to find competitor apps to see if the user problem was already met and solved. My search discovered this was a unique idea that has yet to be created.

The closest thing to this type of idea would be a much bigger platform that called Meetup.

I asked some people who became my user personas if there was an app like this ever built what features would they want it to have that would solve the problems they experience when hiking. They gave me some (listed below)

1. Request friends who hiked your trail the same day. This way you may have talked before and it wouldn't be weird.

2. Having a map of the trail in live view.

2. A chat feature to stay connected to your hiking partners.
